Meet Lola: Steak & Noodle

Where tradition meets taste

Our Story

What started as a small family kitchen in Laoag City has grown into a beloved restaurant. Inspired by grandma’s recipes, we bring warmth and happiness to every plate.

Our Mission

To serve homemade-style meals that make every customer feel at home. Delicious food, served with love.

Our Vision

To be the heart of Laoag City’s dining scene, where every visit leaves a happy memory.
